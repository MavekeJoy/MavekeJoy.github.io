ABOUT THE HOSPITAL

The CJ'S Hospital is a non-profit organization that is leading in medical expertise and service provision, with deservedly earned recognition throughout East Africa and beyond as an advanced diagnostic, treatment and referral centre. Investment in the latest technology and medical equipment has seen us establish leadership in medical procedures both in Kenya and beyond. 


We take pride in our highly qualified professionals who deliver our mission every day, translating their knowledge and expertise to internationally compliant practices in healthcare provision. Courtesy, consideration and unreserved respect towards our patients’ privacy, dignity and confidentiality have helped us earn their trust and goodwill time and again. Our dedicated nursing staff provides care within a friendly and comfortable environment, ensuring a pleasant and anxiety-free time in hospital for our patients and their families.


The CJ'S Hospital Board of Management is in charge of the hospital’s corporate governance and is accountable to the Nation Hospital Association which ensures the hospital’s compliance with the law while maintaining the highest standards of corporate governance and business ethics. The board members attach great importance to the need to conduct the hospital’s business operations with integrity and in accordance with generally accepted corporate practice. They also endorse the internationally developed principles of good governance.
