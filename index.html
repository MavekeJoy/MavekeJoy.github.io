Our Services

DENTISTRY

Our dentists remove tooth decay, fill cavities, and repair fractured teeth. Our dentists diagnose and treat problems with patients' teeth, gums, and related parts of the mouth. They provide advice and instruction on taking care of the teeth and gums and on diet choices that affect oral health.

PHARMACEUTICAL

It provides a wide range of branded and generic medicines, medical devices, personal care products and other basic everyday needs. It aims to be a pharmacy that offers supreme service and easy access to the local residents.

CLINICAL SERVICES

The Directorate of clinical services is an instrumental department in the hospital that delivers Medical, Surgical, Pharmaceutical, Diagnostics, and Public Health & Quality Assurance services. It further monitors the clinical performance throughout the Hospital and promotes the culture of excellence in patient safety, clinical research, training services, quality of care and patient experience. Moreover, we ensure that patients have access to adequate and appropriate medical staff.
